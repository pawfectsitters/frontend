About us

Based in the United Arab Emirates, PawFect Sitters was formed in November 2018 by a group of four animal-loving friends. They set it up after experiencing problems finding reliable petsitters in the UAE. At PawFect Sitters, we specialize in a variety of in-home professional pet sitting services throughout Dubai and Abu Dhabi. From daytime potty breaks to overnight pet sitting, we keep your pet happy, healthy and most importantly, at home. Our range of pet care services are designed to meet your schedule and budget. As your partners in pet care, we understand and respect the unique bond between you and your pet.



Cats

We will visit your cat at home, feed it, refill its water bowl, clean its litter box, play with it and give it a big cuddle (if it wants one).
Our rates: 50 dirhams a day.
Discounts for multiple pet households.

Dogs

We will visit your dog, bring it for a walk or two, play with it, clean up after it, feed it and refill its water bowl.
Our rates: 80 dirhams a day.
Discounts for multiple pet households.

Other domestic pets

We can take care of small animals such as hamsters, guinea pigs and rabbits. We can also handle small to medium sized birds. But no birds of prey please!
Our rates: 50 dirhams a day.
Discounts for multiple pet households.
